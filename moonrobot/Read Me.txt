---------
3D Graphics Robot.
The project was developed in python.
---------
Run the executable - flyingMoonRobot.exe
---------
CONTROLS:
Use these arrow keys to move the robot:
Run - "r"
Camera view up - "w"
Camera view down - "s"
Camera view left - "a"
Camera view right - "d"
Rotate eyes - "c" 
Fly - "space"
Fly forward - "space" and then "r"
Turn left - "o"
Turn Right - "p"
Antenna rotate 1  - "v"
Antenna rotate 2  - "b"
Antenna rotate 3  - "g"
Antenna rotate 4  - "h"
